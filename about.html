About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering graduate from the Government College of Engineering, Yavatmal. My engineering journey is driven by a deep fascination with the intersection of power systems, electronics, and intelligent control. As a recent graduate, I am eager to apply this foundational knowledge across diverse engineering sectors, with a goal of developing robust, efficient, and innovative solutions for complex technical challenges.

What excites me most about electrical engineering is the process of bringing theoretical concepts to life. Whether I am modeling the performance of electrical machines or designing intricate circuit layouts, I approach every challenge with a systems-level perspective. I thrive on bridging the gap between software simulation and physical hardware execution, ensuring that every design is optimized for performance, safety, and reliability. Beyond technical work, I value communication and teamwork. Serving as the Cultural Secretary of the Gymkhana Council and as an active NSS Volunteer has equipped me with the project management skills needed to lead diverse teams and execute complex initiatives effectively.
